THE VISION

Our vision is to create a sustainable service platform to make online trading faster, better and more efficient. Profitable for both private and commercial users with rewards waiting - for protecting the environment and making money in the process.
In this way, we want to make a difference in the world, bring communities together, and relieve and strengthen the environment in various ways.

ABOUT US

The project was funded by Henri Grüger and Julian Gräfen in 2019. Julian Gräfen is 22 years old and studies Code and Context in the fifth semester at the TH-Köln.
Sustainability is particularly important to him, as is efficiency.
Henri Grüger is 21 years old and is currently doing an apprenticeship at the ProfiPress agency.
We both love sustainable and efficient projects. Our goal is to make a positive difference in the world.
